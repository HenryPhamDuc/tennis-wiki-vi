One-Page Cheat Sheet

Effortless Strike System Ready → Load → Tension → 45° → BIC → Finish

1

Ready Position

  • Spine straight, head level high
  • Chest and shoulders slightly forward, hips and butt back
  • Knees softly bent, weight balanced
  • "Compress against the back" feel
2

Load & Align

  • Align body with oncoming ball; run around backhand if needed
  • Turn from facing left to facing right (for right-hander forehand)
  • Left arm + both shoulders create the FRAME/PLANE
  • Right hand must stay on same plane as left arm
  • Pull chest and shoulders back to load
3

Tension & Grip Integration

  • ALWAYS have tension between adjacent joints: hip–shoulder–elbow–wrist–racquet
  • Press racquet head DOWN lightly = pre-load (tensegrity, not stiff)
  • Long axis of racquet in line with forearm, straight arm lever
  • Grip forces:
    • Continental (slice/volley): thumb presses down on top, 3 fingers support below, pull into body CoG
    • Semi-Western (topspin): index knuckle on bevel, index side drives; thumb + 3 fingers counter on opposite bevel to equalize
  • No mid-swing adjustments – face will close automatically
4

Swing Path – 45° Plane

  • Only rotate torso + extend arm, do not "hit"
  • Racquet travels LOW to HIGH on 45-degree plane
  • Whip, do not push or swing with arm
  • Twist around long axis for natural face closure
  • Forehand: left and right arms move together same direction
  • Backhand: arms move opposite to create stretch
5

Contact – BIC

  • Aim at BIC = Bottom Inside Corner of ball
  • Move forward and to left side of ball to align arm and racquet
  • Low ball = more open face, brush up
  • High ball = more closed face, brush down
  • Drive = square face; Spin = angled face
  • Contact happens naturally if structure is set
6

Finish & Options

  • Stay low through follow-through for control (Option 1)
  • Or pivot back foot around and stand up after contact (Option 2)
  • Left arm stays high as anchor then clears left
  • Body rotates around spine, guide ball to target, no extra hit
  • Brain free for tactics: TARGET – FOCUS – COMMIT – EXECUTE
Hit without hitting. Rotation creates power. Tension creates control.
