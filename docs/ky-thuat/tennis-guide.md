Void Rules

When betting on tennis, you may want to consider one big factor, besides the
odds that the book offers you, take into consideration that players may retire
from the match. Depending on the bookmaker, your bet may be voided. There are
four different rules on the market and they are as follows:

1. Ball Served

In order for the bet to stand, one serve is required in the match. This means that if
one of the players decides to retire anytime after the 1st serve, the bet will stand.

2. 1 Set Completed

At least one set needs to be completed in order for the bet to stand.

3. 2 Sets Completed

At least two sets need to be played in order for the bet to stand. If a player reties
before the completion of the 2nd serve, the bet will be voided (cancelled).

4. Match Completed

Entire match has to be completed in order for the bet to stand; retirement anytime
during the match ultimately cancels the bet.
