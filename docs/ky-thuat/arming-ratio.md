# Arming Ratio

Arming Ratio là **diagnostic marker duy nhất xác nhận hoặc bác bỏ SSC failure trong forehand** — không cần video frame-by-frame, không cần sensor: chỉ cần quan sát một moment duy nhất.

> "**The Arming Ratio:** Does the racket head accelerate before the hips have cleared? If the hand passes the plane of the back hip before the navel faces the net, the SSC has failed and the player is pulling with the shoulder rather than whipping from the core. **This is the first fault to identify and correct.**"

---

## Định Nghĩa

Arming Ratio đo **timing relationship** giữa hai events:
1. **Navel clearance**: Thời điểm navel (rốn) faces the net — nghĩa là hips đã fully rotated forward.
2. **Hand passes back hip plane**: Thời điểm hitting hand vượt qua mặt phẳng của back hip.

| Timing | Verdict | Cơ chế |
|---|---|---|
| **Hand passes hip AFTER navel faces net** | ✅ Chain intact | Hips lead → oblique slings release → arm whips from core momentum |
| **Hand passes hip BEFORE navel faces net** | ❌ Arming — SSC failed | Shoulder is pulling arm through, not chain-driven |

---

## Tại Sao Đây Là First Fault To Identify

Arming Ratio là **upstream diagnostic** — nó bắt cả nguồn gốc của lỗi, không phải symptom:

- **Symptom** nhìn thấy: "Flat ball," "no heavy," "wristy finish."
- **Root cause**: Arming ratio sai — hips chưa cleared khi arm fires.

Coaches thường cue về follow-through hoặc wrist khi thấy flat ball. Đây là wrong intervention: **fix the ratio first**.

---

## Progressive Loading Drill — Correction Method

> "**The Arm-Hit Fault** is corrected through the progressive loading drill: hit at 50% focusing entirely on hip drive with the arm passive. At 70%, add the arm's contribution after core initiation. At 100%, the arm should feel like it is catching up to the rotation. This makes the chain sequence **visceral rather than instructional**."

**Ba stages:**

| Stage | Intensity | Focus | Cảm giác mong muốn |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Stage 1** | 50% | Hip drive only; arm completely passive | Bóng đi từ hip rotation, không có arm |
| **Stage 2** | 70% | Add arm contribution **after** core initiation | Arm joins late, hips already moving |
| **Stage 3** | 100% | Arm "catches up" to rotation | Arm feels like a tail being pulled by the body |

"Arm feels like it is catching up to the rotation" là **cảm giác đích** của forehand đúng. Khi player có cảm giác này ở 100% — arming ratio đã được corrected.

---

## Liên Hệ Với Sound Diagnostic

[Slot Quality Sound Diagnostic](slot-quality-sound-diagnostic.md) và Arming Ratio là hai diagnostic tools bổ sung nhau:
- **Sound**: "Thin-slapping" → chain broken (fast, real-time).
- **Arming Ratio**: Visual observation → confirms WHERE chain is broken (diagnostic specificity).

Sound tells you THAT the chain broke. Arming Ratio tells you WHERE: at the hip-arm junction.
