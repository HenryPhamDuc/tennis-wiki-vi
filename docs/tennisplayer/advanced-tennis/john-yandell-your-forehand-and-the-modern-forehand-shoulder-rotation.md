# Shoulder Rotation

This month, we\'ll take a look at another common and controversial technical component: torso rotation. Top players are rotating their shoulders 90 degrees or more in the forward swing on many balls. How does this factor work, when does it happen, and how does it apply to your game?



Shoulder rotation has become extreme in pro tennis, but how does this apply to you?
