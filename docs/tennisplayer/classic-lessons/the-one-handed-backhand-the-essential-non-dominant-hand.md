### Building the One-Handed Backhand:

### Building the One-Handed Backhand:

### The Essential Non-Dominant Hand

### John Craig

This is probably the best analysis ever of how the left or non-dominant hand is critical in a great one-handed backhand. Tried it out myself and loved it! Yes, the right hand and arm have \"all the fun\" in the swing as John puts it.

But it won\'t unless what happens before is pure and correct. The grip change. The turn and racket position, and the counter balance. This emphasis could be just what your one-hander doesn't have. And could transform it!
