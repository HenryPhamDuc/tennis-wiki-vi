# 

### John Craig

In this video you will learn the ideal way to measure your ball toss height and get it perfect every time!

There are many theories on how high the ball toss should be. The truth is most players toss the ball too low, robbing themselves of the time to get balanced and coordinated to make a powerful movement to the ball. Of course tossing the ball too high causes other problems with timing, rhythm and control. So what is the ideal height for most players? You might be surprised by the concept presented in this video.
