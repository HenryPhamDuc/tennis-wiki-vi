### Grip Pressure

### John Craig

Technical patterns are key to every stroke. But the right muscle tension is as well. \"Feel the racket head.\" It\'s a simple concept that solves a question on all the strokes---how tight should you hold the grip? Too loose, you lose control. Too tight you restrict the swing and tighten up, risking injury.

Check out John's solution!
