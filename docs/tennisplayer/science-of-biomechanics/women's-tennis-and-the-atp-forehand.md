**Dr. Brian Gordon**

Dr. Brian Gordon is training high level juniors in Florida and teaching them the ATP forehand, based on his ground breaking research.

Brian believes that whatever the technical state of the forehand on the women\'s tour, women, and especially the juniors he trains can hit a more advanced technical forehand. And he has the players to prove it.
