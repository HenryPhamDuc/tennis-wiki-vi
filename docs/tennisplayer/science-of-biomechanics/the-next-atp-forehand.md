**The Next ATP Forehand?**

**Dr. Brian Gordon**

Tennis is in a state of perpetual evolution. Is that possible true of the ATP forehand? In this article, Dr. Brian Gordon discusses the differences in his original, minimalistic forehand model and the forehands of players like Nick Krygios and Jack Sock.

He explains the differences in the backswings and what that means for the forehand swing, and then shows how he feels new elements could be synthesized with the original ATP fundamentals. Original work from the leading researcher in the world in tennis bio-mechanics.
