### George Zink

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Big inside out forehand. Angle forehand. Attack the backhand. This 5th pattern in George's series combines two elements that you don\'t always see paired. The inside out forehand may be the most commonly hit diagonal in pro tennis and high level junior tennis, but instead of hitting one after another, this pattern follows the inside out with a short crosscourt angle. That combination can open the backhand side quite widely facilitating an attacking finish.
