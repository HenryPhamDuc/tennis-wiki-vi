**Sven Groeneveld:**

### The Value of Working on Your Strengths

### Interviewed By John Yandell

To improve as a player your focus must be on improving your weaknesses, correct? In this second article for Tennisplayer, Sven Groeneveld proposes a different approach. Work on your strength first. By improving your strength you improve not only your results but your confidence and this in turn opens a path to working on your weakness from a positive perspective. This article shares part of the unique approach that has made Sven one of the most successful coaches in tour history. Only on Tennisplayer!
