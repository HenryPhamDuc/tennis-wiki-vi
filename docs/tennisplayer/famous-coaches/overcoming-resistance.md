**Sven Groeneveld:**

**Overcoming Resistance**

### Interviewed By John Yandell

When I heard Sven Groeneveld speak at the Wimbledon coaching conference this June I was particularly struck by his approach to overcoming resistance to coaching ideas from the elite players he has worked with. After his talk I had the chance to ask him to explain it for our subscribers. You may be surprised at what he says. This information is valuable for coaches, but I think it extrapolates to players fighting with themselves over their own barriers to change and improvement.
