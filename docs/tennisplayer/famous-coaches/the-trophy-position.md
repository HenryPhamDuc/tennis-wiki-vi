**Serve Secrets:**

**The Trophy Position**

**Jeff Salzenstein**

In this third and final article in his serve series, Jeff Salzenstein talks about the importance of the trophy position, and the most common errors players make trying to reach it. Then he introduces an original new drill to help you find it perfectly for yourself. Integrated with his previous articles on the starting stance and the arm action for spin, this article helps any player create a blueprint for high level serving.
