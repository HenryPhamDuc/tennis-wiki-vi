### Interviewed by Peter Freeman

------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is an amazingly sophisticated psychological perspective, presented in simple terms by the great Australian champion who won 7 Grand Slam titles. In this interview, John Newcombe outlines his theory of the two minds in tennis. He explains how it is critical to understand both your conscious and subconscious mind and to understand the relationship between them, and how they should communicate in matches.
