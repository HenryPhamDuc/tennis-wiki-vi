# Undulating Periodisation

Undulating Periodisation là model conditioning thay thế [Linear Periodisation Failure](../ky-thuat/linear-periodisation-failure.md) trong ATP calendar: thay vì sequential phases (base → power → peak), tất cả physical qualities được developed **concurrently**, với load và intensity undulating (dao động) theo tournament schedule mỗi tuần.

---

## Nguyên Lý Cốt Lõi

### Concurrent Training
Thay vì "build strength first, then power, then sport-specific" — player train tất cả qualities simultaneously mỗi tuần:
- **Explosive power** (plyometrics, medicine ball, sprint intervals).
- **Neuromuscular strength** (micro-loads, isometric holds).
- **Eccentric deceleration capacity** (braking mechanics, labral protection).
- **Aerobic maintenance** (enough to support recovery between efforts — not primary focus).

Concurrent training nghĩa là không có một quality nào bị neglected trong tournament block — không có "I'll work on strength in the off-season" thinking.

### Undulating Load — Tournament-Week Structure

| Week Type | Volume | Intensity | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Preparation block** (3–4 tuần) | High | High | Full development sessions — plyometrics, strength, sprint intervals |
| **Tournament week** | Very low | Low-moderate | [Micro-Loading](../ky-thuat/micro-loading.md) only — activation, no accumulative fatigue |
| **Post-tournament recovery** | Low | Low | CNS recovery — movement and light technical work |
| **Development block** (between tournaments) | High | High | Return to full development |

Undulation means the programme adapts to **what the calendar demands**, not what the linear model prescribes.

## 12-Week Template — Ivancevic Framework (2026 Updated)

The programme adapted from the Ivancevic et al. 12-week tennis performance framework integrates **three simultaneous tracks**:

### Track 1 — Physical Conditioning
- Weeks 1–4: Sprint interval foundation, [Eccentric Deceleration Training](../ky-thuat/eccentric-deceleration-training.md), plyometric base.
- Weeks 5–8: [Type IIb Fast-Twitch Optimisation](../ky-thuat/type-iib-fast-twitch-optimisation.md), contrast training (heavy → explosive same session).
- Weeks 9–12: Match-intensity simulation, [Micro-Loading](../ky-thuat/micro-loading.md) maintenance, CNS monitoring intensified.

### Track 2 — Technical Development
- Stroke technical work concurrent with physical — not separated.
- **Key principle**: Technical work done under physical fatigue = match-realistic training. Pure technical work in fresh state = practice-only engrams.

### Track 3 — Tactical & Cognitive
- [Neural Pressure Training](../tam-ly/neural-pressure-training.md) — chess work, decision drills under fatigue — concurrent with physical.
- Pattern drilling integrated into conditioning circuits (hit forehand pattern → sprint recovery → repeat).

---

## Tournament-Week Protocol — Critical Detail

Biggest linear periodisation error: treating tournament week like a normal training week with match play added on top.

**2026 standard**: Tournament week = [Micro-Loading](../ky-thuat/micro-loading.md) only:
- Day before match: 15–20 min activation, no loading.
- Day after first-round match: Recovery protocol, not standard training session.
- Between consecutive day matches: CNS-only work.

"The second model [standard training + match play] produces CNS Fatigue by the fourth day of a tournament."
