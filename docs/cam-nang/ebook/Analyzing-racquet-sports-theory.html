General Theory (Racquet Power)

Swing Flow

1

Physics Core ( Bội Âm = Harmony )

  • The more harmonics, the higher f (frequency/pulse)
  • Fast pulse = stronger resonant force from racquet head
  • In 1 period = 1 sine wave; ½ sine wave ⇒ amplitude ×2
  • Implication: short, sharp impulse beats long push
2

Swing Loop

  • Take back to High Point
  • Hit DOWN right after high point — Hard & Quick
  • Racquet head rebounds and accelerates (“bị bật lại”)
  • Add body rotation, keep accelerating
  • Key control: angle, not muscle
3

Launch Geometry (FH, BH, Serve)

  • ▸ HIGH ball: racquet BEHIND legs, more open face, raise launch angle
  • ▸ FLAT ball: racquet IN FRONT of legs, more closed face, smaller launch angle
  • ▸ Prep: keep racquet head closed, tip pointed forward to ball
4

Contact Path

  • Hit THROUGH center = Flat = Down-The-Line
  • Hit ACROSS upper-outside = Topspin = Cross-Court
  • Change face angle vs ground = change spin
  • Position: more behind right leg = low-to-high lift; more in front = drive
5

Footwork & Recovery

  • Move with reverse / karioka steps
  • Circle recovery, chest facing center
  • 1→2: Run Around
  • 2→3: Little adjustment steps

After 3: read opponent

  • •C-C return → move to net for volley
  • •D-T-L return → move to middle to intercept
